Opening Hours!

Rendering your Opening Hours has never been so easy

Based on jQuery, MomentJS and JSON this library gives you the possibility to render your Business's Opening Hours in just few steps

Tell me more

Scenario - owner of a local business

You are the owner of a Restaurant and you want to be showing the opening hours of your local business on the contact page of your website. Furthermore, you might have different opening hours based on the season (e.g. between January-June you have different Opening Hours as from Juli-September, etc). You don't have the time to go on your website and change it? OpeningHours.js is right here for you!

Follow the steps described here and let the library do the work for you!

Write once, use all the time!

Step 1: add the libraries at the top of your HTML document (inside the head section)

<link rel="stylesheet" href="OpeningHours.css" type="text/css" />

<script type="text/javascript" src="http://code.jquery.com/jquery-2.1.0.min.js"></script>

<script type="text/javascript" src="moment-with-langs.js"></script>

<script type="text/javascript" src="OpeningHours.js"></script>

Step 2: Create the JSON with your Opening Hours

{ "weekPeriod": [ { "dayOfWeek": "Monday", "opens": "HH:MM:SS", "closes": "HH:MM:SS" }, ... ] }
